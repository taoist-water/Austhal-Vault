# The Endeavour Catalog
### Tend to the Flesh (Physical Recovery)

Characters spend their time stitched up, drinking foul tonics, or sweating out fever in a filthy cot.

- The Check: Wits + Medicine or Prowess + Constitution vs TN 8.
    
- Resolution Results:    

	- Snake Eyes: The wound turns gangrenous. Mark 1 permanent Dissonant Stress slot that cannot be cleared until a Master-level physician operates.
    
	- Failure: Bandages slip, infection sets in. No Wounds are cleared.
    
	- Success: You successfully clear 2 filled Wound slots.
    
	- Massive Success (12+): Pure medical expertise. Clear all physical Wound slots, and your next health-based check in the next adventure gains Advantage.

### Ground the Spirit / Carouse (Stress Relief)

Arcanists purge backlashed energy from their minds; martials lose themselves in gambling dens, churches, or cheap ale to wash away the horrors of combat.

- The Check: Will or Wits (depending on if it's Arcane Grounding or Mundane Carousing) vs TN 8.
    
- Resolution Results:    

	- Snake Eyes: You cross a dangerous line. Arcanists suffer a permanent psychological tic; martials wake up in a gutter missing half their starting silver.
    
	- Failure: The nightmares persist. No Stress slots are unlocked or cleared.
    
	- Success: Clear up to 3 slots of combined Locked or Dissonant Stress.
    
	- Massive Success (12+): Complete mental restoration. Wipe your entire Stress tracker clean. You start the next adventure with 1 free banked Momentum from your newfound clarity.

###  Forage & Haggle (Sourcing Rare Gear)

Looking for replacements for broken shields, high-tier armor pieces, or specialized alchemical components.

- The Rule: The item's Availability dictates the base difficulty modifier set by the GM (e.g., Common = +0, Scarce = -2, Rare = -4). The size of the settlement can shift this tier up or down.
    
- The Check: Wits + Insight/Scouting vs TN 8 + Modifiers.
    
- Resolution Results:    

	- Failure: The item simply cannot be sourced in this market.
    
	- Success: You find a merchant stocking the exact trapping. You may purchase it for standard list price.
    
	- Massive Success (12+): You locate a desperate seller or strike a brilliant deal. You acquire the item at 25% off list price, or find an additional rare component tucked away in their wares.

### Hammer & Forge (Repairing Shoddy or Broken Gear)

Taking a hammer to dented plates or re-weaving a shattered chain shirt using workshop tools.

- The Check: Prowess + Repair vs TN 8.
    
- Resolution Results:    

	- Snake Eyes: The item fractures irrevocably. It is permanently broken beyond repair.
    
	- Success: You repair 1 piece of damaged armor or remove the "Shoddy" tag from a weapon.
    
	- Massive Success (12+): Exceptional craftwork. You repair the armor and reinforce a structural weak point, giving it a flat +1 bonus to its structural integrity for its first hit in the upcoming adventure.

### THE CRAFTING ENGINE (Crafting & Wits)

Crafting shouldn't be a tedious spreadsheet simulator. It should be a tactical use of downtime.

- The Mechanic: Crafting requires tools, raw materials (represented simply by Gold/Loot value), and Time (Downtime). The player rolls 2d6 + Wits + Crafting against TN 8.
    
- Outcomes:    

	- Success: The item (Alchemical Draught, trap, armor, weapon) is created.
    
	- Massive Success (Margin 4+): You work with extreme efficiency. You either halve the time required, or salvage materials (halving the Gold cost).
	
	- Failure: The time is spent, but the materials are ruined.

###  Study a Mark (Tactical Preparation)

Directly adapted from WFRP’s Rogue Endeavours. Stalking a noble house, researching old archives about a dungeon, or studying regional maps.

- The Check: Wits + Investigation or Wits + Lore vs TN 8.
    
- Resolution Results:    

	- Success: You uncover vital tactical information. You gain a generic "Prep Tag" specific to that target (e.g., Knows Hidden Sewer Entrance or Aware of Drake's Weak Belly Scale). Burning this tag during the upcoming adventure instantly grants your party Advantage on an engagement or clash check.
    
	- Massive Success (12+): Flawless recon. You gain two different Prep Tags, and your entire party receives a +1 bonus to their Draw (Initiative) check during the first round of the confrontation because you completely predicted their deployment.
