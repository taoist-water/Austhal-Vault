# Domains
Faith domains represent direct divine intervention powered by rigid devotion. Faith casters do not lock stress but are subject to the immediate rolls against stability. Their domains give them an aura and a specific prayer manifestation.

#### The Domain of Strategy (The Cult of the Iron Horizon)

- Flavor/embellishments: Pristine polished iron shields, symbols of spears and eagles, clean cut and disciplined; prayers manifest as subtle tactical commands that echo clearly over the din of battle.
    
- Domain Tag (Tactical Horizon): Whenever you successfully pass a Faith check vs. TN 8 to cast a prayer, you can choose to grant 1 point of Momentum to an ally instead gaining a personal benefit, symbolizing divine tactical insight layout.    

#### The Domain of Trickery (The Cult of the Crooked Coin)

- Flavor/embellishments: Loaded dice amulets, mismatched clothes, soft leather gloves; prayers are murmured riddles, sly smiles, and localized distortions of luck.
    
- Domain Tag (Fickle Fate): When an ally within 10ft rolls a Fumble (Snake Eyes), you can spend 1 point of your own Stress as a Reaction to turn the result into a standard failure instead. You take the burden of their bad luck.
    

#### The Domain of Law (The Cult of the Zenith)

- Flavor/embellishments: Heavy pendants of merchant scales, heavy maces, unyielding loud booming scripture; prayers are backed by thunderclaps and searing white light.
    
- Domain Tag (Smite Corruption): Your offensive prayers gain a righteous edge. When targeting Undead, Daemons, or Mutants, your spells treat the target's Wound Threshold [T] as 1 point lower than normal due to divine purging.
    

#### The Domain of Death (The Cult of the Ashen Veil)

- Flavor/embellishments: Black hooded raiment, stark quiet expressions, scents of river mud and white lilies; prayers manifest as chilling quietude, fading lights, and falling feathers.
    
- Domain Tag (Rest in Peace): When you use the Stabilize prayer or cast a ward on a dying ally, the target becomes completely immune to further Stress gains from mental shock, fear tags, or supernatural dread for the remainder of the scene.
    

#### The Domain of Winter & Wilds (The Cult of the Rime-Fang)

- Flavor/embellishments: Heavy white wolf pelts, unkempt beards, frosted breath even indoors. Prayers manifest as freezing howling wind, icy frost coating weapons, or bloodthirsty wolf howls.
    
- Domain Tag (Chilling Frost): When you successfully cast an offensive prayer (Winter's Bite), the target is numbed by supernatural cold. Their movement is restricted: they cannot perform a Move action on their next turn unless they choose to accept 1 point of physical Stress to force their frozen muscles to snap free.
    

#### The Domain of Mercy & Healing (The Cult of the Weeping Martyr)

- Flavor/embellishments Plain white habits, dove pendants, soft soothing hands that never touch weapons willingly. Prayers manifest as golden tears, warm glowing auras, and a deep psychological sense of peace.
    
- Domain Tag (Pure Martyrdom): When you cast a Healing or Stabilize prayer on a critically injured ally, you can opt to transfer their agony to your own spirit. By taking 1 point of Locked Stress yourself, you cause your prayer to clear an additional Wound Slot on the target creature.
    

#### The Domain of the Sea & Storms (The Tidespoken Clergy)

- Flavor/embellishments: Sea-shell tokens, salt-crusted oilskins, trident wooden focuses. Prayers manifest as the crash of distant rogue waves, heavy brine smells, or high-pressure water arcs.
    
- Domain Tag (Tidal Undertow): Whenever you successfully affect an enemy with a prayer, you can command the sea's pull. The target is physically shoved 5 ft in a direction of your choosing, altering positioning tags and breaking close engagement traps.
