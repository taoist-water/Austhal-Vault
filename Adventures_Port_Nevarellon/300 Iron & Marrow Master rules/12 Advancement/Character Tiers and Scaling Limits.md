# Character Tiers & Scaling Limits

As characters earn Development Points via narrative milestones, their cumulative DP spent pushes them into higher tiers. The caps on their maximum attributes and skill layers shift predictably alongside their growth.

'Novice Tier'--------> 'Journeyman Tier'---------> 'Adept Tier' --------> 'Master Tier'

 8 - 19 Total DP          20 - 31 Total DP          32 - 43 Total DP         44+ Total DP

### I. Novice (8–19 Total DP Spent)

- The Vibe: You are an ordinary peasant, an apprentice scribe, or a desperate cutthroat thrust into terrible circumstances. You rely heavily on teamwork and positioning because your personal math is highly volatile.
    
- Mechanical Caps: Attributes are strictly capped at a maximum of 3. Skills are capped at the Novice Tier.
    

### II. Journeyman (20–31 Total DP Spent)

- The Vibe: You have hardened your body and refined your focus. You are a seasoned mercenary, a hedge-mage who has survived backlashes, or a veteran scout.
    
- Mechanical Caps: The attribute ceiling rises to a maximum of 4. You unlock the ability to purchase Journeyman Tier Skills (4 DP).
    

### III. Adept (32–43 Total DP Spent)

- The Vibe: You are a dangerous individual whose physical or mental conditioning has pushed past normal human boundaries.
    
- Mechanical Caps: The absolute human peak opens up; you can advance a single primary Attribute to its absolute maximum of 5. Skills remain capped at the Journeyman level, representing a focus on raw attribute perfection over final technical execution.
    

### IV. Master (44+ Total DP Spent)

- The Vibe: A legendary paragon of your craft. Your techniques are flawless, and your mind can handle the most volatile magical matrixes without shattering.
    
- Mechanical Caps: You unlock the ability to purchase Master Tier Skills (6 DP) and Tier 3 Capstone Feats like Rote Manifestation. Attributes remain capped at 5.
