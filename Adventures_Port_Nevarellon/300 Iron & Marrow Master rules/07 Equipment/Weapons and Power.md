# Weapons & Power

Weapon damage in this system is calculated as Impact = (Winner Roll - Loser Roll) + Weapon Power. 

- Power 0 (Unarmed/Improvised): Raw kinetic impact. Cannot cause Wounds without specific feats (like Lethal Strikes).
    
- Power 1 (Light/Small): Daggers, shortswords, hand axes.
    
- Power 2 (Standard/Martial): Longswords, battleaxes, maces, spears.
    
- Power 3 (Heavy/Two-Handed): Greatswords, halberds, mauls.
