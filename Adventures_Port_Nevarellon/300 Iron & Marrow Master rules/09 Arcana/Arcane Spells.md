# Arcane Spells

### CANTRIPS (Cost: 0 Stress | Unmodifiable)

- Force Bolt (Aggressor Action): Requires Wand or empty hand. Roll Wits + Arcana. Power 0. A raw concussive blast. (If using a Wand, the caster may spend 1 Stress to apply Elemental Shift for special weapon tags).
    
- Mage Hand: Telekinetically manipulate an object weighing up to 10 lbs within 30 feet.
    
- Minor Illusion: Create a static hologram or a disembodied sound (e.g., footsteps) to distract guards or force an enemy to roll their Engagement Draw with Disadvantage if they are startled by it.
    
- Flare / Chill: Cause a small object to instantly combust or freeze solid.
    

### NOVICE SPELLS (Cost: 1 Stress)

- Project Force (Aggressor Action): Hurl a concentrated mass of fire, ice, or void. Wits + Arcana vs Reflex. Power 2. (Spend 1 Momentum to add the Area of Effect tag).
    
- Deflect (Reaction): Cast instantly when you or an adjacent ally are attacked. Roll Wits + Arcana. If your roll beats the incoming attack, you weave a temporary kinetic barrier. It grants a Shield Value (SV) equal to your Margin of Success for that single impact.
    
- Obscuring Mist (Action): Fill a 20x20 ft area with dense fog. All ranged attacks passing through it suffer Disadvantage. Engagement Draws inside the fog rely on Wits instead of Reflex.
    
- Gravity Well (Action): You dramatically increase the local gravity in a 15ft radius. Any enemy starting their turn inside or entering the area must make a Prowess check or their Pace is reduced to a crawl, and they suffer Disadvantage on Reflex rolls.
    

### ADEPT SPELLS (Cost: 2 Stress)

- Elemental Burst (Aggressor Action): A devastating eruption. Roll Wits + Arcana vs the Reflex of everyone in a 20ft radius. Power 4. (Highly dangerous to allies without the Arcane Architect feat).
    
- Haste / Sluggish (Action): Bend the flow of time around a single target.
    

- Ally: Automatically wins their next Engagement Draw and gains Advantage on all Reflex rolls.
    
- Enemy (Opposed by Will): Automatically loses all Engagement Draws and suffers Disadvantage on Reflex rolls.
    

- Blink (Move Action/Reaction): Fold space. Instantly teleport up to 50 feet to a location you can see, bypassing all terrain, Engagement rules, and Attacks of Opportunity.
    
- Shatter (Action): Target a specific inanimate object or piece of armor. Roll Wits + Arcana vs a target number based on the material (e.g., 7 for wood, 9 for iron). On a success, the object violently explodes. If targeting worn armor, permanently reduce the armor's bonus by 1d3.
    

### MASTER SPELLS (Cost: 3 Stress | Absolute Exertion)

- Disintegrate (Aggressor Action): A beam of pure annihilation. Wits + Arcana vs Reflex. Power 4. If this hits, completely ignore the target's Armour when calculating their Wound Threshold. If it inflicts a Wound, any armor they are wearing is permanently destroyed.
    
- Stop Time (Action): The world freezes. You immediately take two full consecutive turns. You cannot directly attack an enemy while time is stopped, but you can cast buffs, heal, set traps, or retreat.
