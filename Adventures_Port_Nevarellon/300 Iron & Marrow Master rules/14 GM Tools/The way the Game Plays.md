The overall game is a string of Scenes, created by the GM and strung together to form a story.



Players take turns Activating their character and taking actions. Outside of combat, such as exploring the world or socially engaging actions are either played out like an RPG and when required taking an action that requires some dice rolling.  Usually against a [[Target Number]], the result determines the outcome of the action.  Sometimes these actions will be Opposed by an opponent, meaning dice are rolled, appropriate modifiers are added and compared to the roll of the opponent.  Whoever rolls highest wins.  Depending on the action taken could also determine how well or how poorly a character has performed in this opposed roll.
