# The Glitch (Baseline Arcana Rule): 
We can bake this directly into the Laws of Arcana as a universal rule for high-level casting.
    
- Implementation: When casting an Adept or Master spell, if the check results in at least one natural 1, the spell still succeeds if the total hits the Target Number [TN], but the caster suffers +2 Dissonant Stress as the energies "leak". This ensures the "Death Spiral" is always looming, without making the caster feel incompetent.
