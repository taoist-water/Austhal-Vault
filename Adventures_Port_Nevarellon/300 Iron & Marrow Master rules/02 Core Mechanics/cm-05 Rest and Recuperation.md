# \[cm-05\] Rest and Recuperation

## Short Rest:
a period of downtime, lasting at least 1 hour, during which a character does nothing more strenuous than eating, drinking, reading, or tending to wounds. 

## Long Rest:
a period of downtime, lasting at least typically 8 hours, during which a character does nothing more strenuous than eating, drinking, uninterrupted sleeping.
