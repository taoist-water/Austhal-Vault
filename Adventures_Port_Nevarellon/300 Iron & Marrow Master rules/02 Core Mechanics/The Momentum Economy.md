
In _Iron & Marrow_, **Momentum** is the central tactical currency that dictates the ebb and flow of survival. It is the mechanical representation of adrenaline, battlefield positioning, psychological dominance, and the capitalization on an enemy's mistakes.

## The Bank: 
- Max 5 Momentum. Resets to 0 at the end of combat.
- By default, this bank resets to 0 at the end of a scene.
## Gaining Momentum


Gaining Momentum:

- (+1) Highest Activation Initiative at the start of a round 
    

- (+1) Winning a Clash  as an Aggressor 
    
- (+1) Win a Clash as a Reactor by a margin of 3+ .
    
- (+1) Successful Tactical Assessment Action: 2d6 + Wits+Skill 
    
- (+1) Winning the draw by 5+
    
- (+2) Winning the Draw by 12+
