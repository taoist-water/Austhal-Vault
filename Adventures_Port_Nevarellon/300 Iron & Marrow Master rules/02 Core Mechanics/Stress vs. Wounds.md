## Stress:
- Stress is categorised as either Dissonant or Locked. 
- Dissonant Stress (gained from trauma, fumbles, or exhaustion) applies a cumulative -1 penalty per point to all subsequent rolls. 
    - cleared by spending Momentum, taking a short rest or use of items.
- Locked Stress (gained from casting Arcane spells, conditions,etc) does not apply this penalty, though it still counts against your Stress Limit.
    -  cleared by Grounding action, or full nights rest, Downtime activity.
## Wounds:
- represent physical trauma. 
- A character has 3 wound slots. 
- Taking a 4th wound means you are Incapacitated.
-  Wounds do not apply stat penalties, but require medical supplies or magic to heal ( 1 wound per 3 days rest, or via Medicine check).

## Short Rest:
a period of downtime, lasting at least 1 hour, during which a character does nothing more strenuous than eating, drinking, reading, or tending to wounds. 

## The Death Spiral: 
if a character's Stress limit is maxed out, any further Stress instantly converts into Wounds.
