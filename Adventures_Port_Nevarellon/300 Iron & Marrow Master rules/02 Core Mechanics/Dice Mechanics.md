# \[cm-02\] Dice Mechanics

- The Check: 2d6 + Attribute + Skill vs. TN 8 (or Opposed).
- The Desperate Edge (Exploding 6s): When a natural 6 is rolled, the player (or Elite NPC) may choose to roll an additional die and add it to the total. This can be repeated as long as a 6 is rolled. However, if at any point during the initial roll or the explosion chain the dice show two natural 1s, the entire action collapses into a Fumble.
- Fumble: Automatic failure. The character immediately suffers 3 Stress.
