## The Engagement Flow
1. Activation: 2d6 + Reflex (Determines turn order).
	- Activations are then executed in descending order.
	- once all Activations are complete, the round is over.
	- All participants roll for Activation order again.
    
2. Move and Engage: Active Characters MAY move into an enemies threat zone and become engaged.
    
3. Engagement (The Draw): Both roll 2D6 + Reflex. Winner is the  Aggressor; Loser is the  Reactor
    - Engaging with ranged: When engaging a target outside of the target's Move value [[MV]], there is no Engagement roll. The target is automatically the Reactor.
    
4. In a Tie: the weapons bind the engagement ends 
    
5. Declarations: Reactor declares defence (e.g., Dodge, Block) first; Aggressor declares attack (e.g., Strike, Feint).
    
6. Fight (The Clash): Both roll chosen actions, Highest total wins. Compare roll results.
    - In a Tie: the weapons bind the clash ends and both participants take 1 Stress.  Heroes always break ties against FODDER level enemies, resolve as if the Hero has resulted 1 higher than the FODDER minion.

## Combat on the Grid
- Scale: 1 Square = 5ft. Medium creatures occupy 1 square and move 30 ft (6 squares) per action.
    
- Threat zone: Threaten all adjacent 8 squares (5ft radius).
    
- Provoking: Leaving a Threat Zone normally grants the enemy a Free Aggressor action - strike with Advantage.
    
- The Swarm Bonus (outnumbered): For each additional ally engaging the same target, attackers gain +1 to Engagement Draw and +1 to Clash rolls (Max +2).

## Damage Calculation
Impact: (Winner Roll - Loser Roll) + Weapon Power + any other modifiers

- Impact < Threshold: 1 Stress.
    
- Impact => Threshold: 1 Minor Wound (1 slot) +1 stress.
    
- Impact => 2x Threshold: 1 Major Wound (2 slots) +2 stress.
    
- Impact => 3x Threshold: Incapacitated. Potential long term injury.
