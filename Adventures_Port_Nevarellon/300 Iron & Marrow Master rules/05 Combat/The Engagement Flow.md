1. Activation: 2d6 + Reflex (Determines turn order).
    
2. Move and Engage: Active Characters MAY move into an enemies threat zone and become engaged.
    
3. Engagement (The Draw): Both roll 2D6 + Reflex. Winner is the  Aggressor; Loser is the  Reactor
    

4. Engaging with ranged: When engaging a target outside of the target's Move value [MV], the target is automatically the Reactor.
    
5. In a Tie: the weapons bind the engagement ends 
    

6. Declarations: Reactor declares defense (e.g., Dodge, Block) first; Aggressor declares attack (e.g., Strike, Feint).
    
7. Fight (The Clash): Both roll chosen actions, Highest total wins. Compare roll results.
    

8. In a Tie: the weapons bind the clash ends and both participants take 1 Stress.  Heroes always break ties against FODDER level enemies, resolve as if the Hero has resulted 1 higher than the FODDER minion.
