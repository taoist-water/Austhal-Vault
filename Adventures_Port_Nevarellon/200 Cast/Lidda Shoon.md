# Character: Lidda Shoon
#cast/active #status/solid

> "She'll appraise your mother's wedding ring, tell you it's worthless brass, buy it for a copper, and sell the melted gold to a High Quarter clerk by noon." 

## 📊 Vital Statistics
- **Full Name / Aliases:** Lidda Shoon
- **Current Occupation:** "Average" Jeweler / Syndicate Fence / Minor Thief.
- **Race/Faith:** Halfling. A devout, quiet follower of the **Cult of the Crooked Coin** (The Domain of Trickery). 
- **Social Class / Standing:** Un-Landed Citizen (Rents her jeweler's booth).
- **Primary Hangout:** [[000 Atlas/The Rusty Tankard|The Rusty Tankard]].

## ⚖️ Realism & Physicality
- **The Front:** She operates a highly unremarkable, middling jewelry appraisal stand in the artisan markets. It is incredibly boring by design. 
- **The Reality:** Lidda is the Syndicate's primary physical fence. When Cobalt Feather agents steal High Quarter signet rings, silver cutlery, or gem-encrusted lockets, they cannot be sold openly. Lidda uses her jeweler's tools to pry out the stones, melt down the traceable metals into untraceable ingots, and distribute the clean wealth back into the network.
- **Cult Ties:** As a follower of the Crooked Coin, she views her counterfeiting and fencing not just as a job, but as a minor religious sacrament—a way of rebalancing the unfair wealth of the city through localized distortions of luck and law.
