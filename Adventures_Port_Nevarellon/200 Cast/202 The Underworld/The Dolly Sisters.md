## 🏛️ Clara Dolly (The Business)
### ⚖️ Realism & Physicality 
- **Age & Vitality:** 42 years old. She carries herself with severe, rigid posture. The salt air has hardened her skin, and she suffers from chronic rheumatism in her right hand, causing her to constantly massage her knuckles. 
- **Financial Status:** Exceptionally wealthy in liquid coin, though she hides it aggressively. She dresses in plain, dark-dyed woolens to avoid attracting the attention of thieves on the boardwalks.
- **Equipment:** Carries a heavy ring of iron keys pinned to her hip and a small, razor-sharp ledger-knife tucked into her sleeve. 
### 🧠 Psychology & Drive 
- **Immediate Goal:** Secure a cheaper supply of fresh water for her staff; the current municipal water-tax is eating too deep into their weekly margins.
- **The Core Fear:** A structural collapse of the old ship hull during the winter storm surges, which would drop her iron coin vaults into the harbor silt.
- **Moral Compromises:** She regularly blackmails mid-level merchants using details her hostesses gather, viewing it as a simple cost of doing business. 



## 🏛️ Tessa Dolly (The Eyes & Ears)
### ⚖️ Realism & Physicality 
- **Age & Vitality:** 38 years old. Soft-spoken, sharp-eyed, and far more expressive than her sister. She smokes a cheap, bitter pipe-weed constantly to mask the damp stench of the bilge below. 
- **Financial Status:** Keeps no personal coin; all her earnings go straight into Clara's centralized vault. She measures her wealth entirely in leverage and secrets. 
- **Equipment:** A silver pocket-watch (a gift from a foreign captain) that runs four minutes slow, and a small vial of powdered [[Nightshade|nightshade]] concealed in a hollow ring. 
### 🧠 Psychology & Drive 
- **Immediate Goal:** Keep [[Silas Bane|Silas Bane]] from starting a turf war with the Alchemists inside her lounge; dead enforcers ruin the upholstery and bring the Watch. 
- **The Core Fear:** Being exposed by the Syndicate as an independent broker. If [[Garrick the Keelhauler|Garrick]] realizes how much information she sells *outside* the gang, she will end up in the mud. 
- **Moral Compromises:** She purposefully plies young, naive sailors with spiked wine to extract cargo manifests and shipping timetables.

## 🤫 Shared Backstory & The Core Secret 
Originally from the squalid farming villages outside the city, the sisters arrived in Port Nevarellon twenty years ago with nothing. They survived by converting a derelict ship hull into the port's most secure lounge.
### 💎 The Black Ledger (The Vault Secret) 
The sisters do not just collect secrets for survival—they are running a massive extortion ring against the city's elite. Hidden behind a false bulkhead in their private cabin is a leather-bound journal written in Clara's precise code. It contains the names of several prominent nobles from **The High Quarter** who are secretly financing illegal smuggling operations through **The Iron-Anchor Syndicate** to avoid paying the [[Council of Five]]'s maritime tariffs. This ledger is their ultimate life insurance policy; if either sister is murdered, a designated courier has instructions to drop the journal directly on the steps of the High Court.
