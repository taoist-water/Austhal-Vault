## 📊 Vital Statistics
- **Full Name / Aliases:** Kaleb / "The Lock-Smith" (Old alias from her younger days).
- **Current Occupation:** Barkeep and Manager of [[000 Atlas/The Rusty Tankard|The Rusty Tankard]].
- **Social Class / Standing:** Un-Landed Citizen (Living on a managed commercial lease).
- **Affiliations:** [[100 Society/The Cobalt Feather Syndicate|The Cobalt Feather Syndicate]] (Liaison / Safehouse Keeper).

---

## ⚖️ Realism & Physicality
- **Age & Vitality:** Human, 51 years old. His body is heavily worn by a lifetime of hard labor and street-level survival, but he possesses a quiet, deceptive physical density.
- **Physical Flaws / Limitations:** He walks with a pronounced stiffness in his left knee—the result of it being shattered by an iron truncheon during a Dock-Watch raid twenty years ago. His hands are thick, heavily calloused, and missing the tip of his right index finger.
- **Financial Status:** Officially broke; his daily ledger shows he barely makes enough coppers to pay the property lease to the High Quarter clerk. In reality, he receives a steady, untraceable income of **Silver Pieces** from the Cobalt Feather for maintaining the cellar drop and keeping his mouth shut.

---

## 🧠 Psychology & Drive
- **Immediate Goal:** Keep the peace in the Tankard. If a fight breaks out and draws the attention of the Golden Company, it risks exposing the cellar dead-drop.
- **The Core Fear:** The [[100 Society/The Iron-Anchor Syndicate|Iron-Anchor Syndicate]] discovering his true affiliation. He knows Garrick would gladly hang him from a harbor crane to send a message to the Cobalt Feather leadership.
- **The Front:** Kaleb plays the role of a tired, slightly deaf, grumbling old barkeep perfectly. He hears every rumor that passes through the Basin—which merchant galleons are carrying unregistered gold, which Golden Company captains are taking bribes, and which scribes are deep in debt—and passes it upward through the Feather's network.
