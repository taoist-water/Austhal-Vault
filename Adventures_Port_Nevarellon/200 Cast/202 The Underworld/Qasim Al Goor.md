# Character: Qasim Al Goor
#cast/active #status/solid

> "If the water is deeper than a man's knee, Qasim can sail a boat through it. If it's shallower than that, he'll just put the boat on his back and carry it." — Maccorrack

## 📊 Vital Statistics
- **Full Name / Aliases:** Qasim Al Goor
- **Current Occupation:** Skipper of *The Curzon*.
- **Social Class / Standing:** Un-Landed Citizen (Technically a shipmaster, but *The Curzon* is not a deep-water keel, denying him Landed status).
- **Affiliations:** [[100 Society/The Cobalt Feather Syndicate|The Cobalt Feather Syndicate]] (Smuggler).

## ⚖️ Realism & Physicality
- **The Vessel:** *The Curzon* is a shallow-hulled, flat-bottomed river ship rigged with specialized marsh-sails. It is practically useless in the cataclysmic storms of the open ocean, but it is a masterwork of engineering for navigating the shifting sandbars, coastal shallows, and the "inside passages" of the Whispering Coast.
- **Operations:** Qasim is the logistical backbone of Alfric's inland network. He moves high-value, low-weight contraband (forged documents, untaxed gold, rare alchemical reagents) up the riverlands, completely bypassing the Golden Company's heavy naval blockades at the mouth of the Great Anchor Basin.
