# Character: Maccorrack
#cast/active #status/solid

> "He’s built like a siege engine and has the blood of the old wars in his veins, but he asks for his ale with the manners of a temple priest." — Kaleb, Barkeep of the Rusty Tankard

## 📊 Vital Statistics
- **Full Name / Aliases:** Maccorrack
- **Current Occupation:** Stevedore (Dockhand) / Syndicate Muscle.
- **Race:** Half-Orc (Descendant of the manufactured monstrous weapons of the pre-sundering war).
- **Social Class / Standing:** Un-Landed Citizen / Escaped Slave.
- **Primary Residence:** A hammock in a damp storage loft above [[000 Atlas/The Rusty Tankard|The Rusty Tankard]].

## ⚖️ Realism & Physicality
- **Physicality:** Massive, heavily scarred, and visibly carrying the genetic markers of the monstrous races (heavy jaw, dense bone structure). Due to the city's extreme prejudice and the laws of the [[100 Society/Law - The Council's Edicts|Palm-Length Edict]], he relies entirely on his fists and heavy wooden shipping crates in a fight.
- **Physical Flaws / Limitations:** Maccorrack suffers from a severe narcotics dependency—likely a low-frequency marsh-root or brine-lotus—used to numb the psychological trauma of his past and the physical aches of heavy labor. 
- **Background:** He is an escaped slave who fled north from the brutal [[100 Society/The Chalced Kingdom|Chalced Kingdom]]. The trauma of his enslavement has stripped him of the typical berserker rage expected of his lineage; instead, he is uncharacteristically soft-spoken, deliberate, and fiercely loyal to those who treat him like a man rather than a beast.

## 🧠 Psychology & Drive
- **Immediate Goal:** Earn enough silver from dock-wages and underground brawls at the Rusty Tankard to feed his narcotic habit and keep a roof over his head.
- **The Core Fear:** Being recognized by a southern Retriever and dragged back in chains to the Chalced Kingdom.
- **Current Role:** He works as a legitimate stevedore by day. By night, Alfric and Kaleb use him as a bodyguard. When the Syndicate trades stolen goods in the hostile pirate towns of the Silted Marshes, Maccorrack is brought along as silent, imposing insurance.
