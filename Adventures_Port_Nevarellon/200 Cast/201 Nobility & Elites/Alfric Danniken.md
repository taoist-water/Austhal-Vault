# Character: Alfric Danniken
#cast/active #status/solid

> "He wears a silk doublet and calls himself a lord, but if you look closely at his ledger, the ink smells like marsh-water and stolen spice." — Master Scribe Vaelen

## 📊 Vital Statistics
- **Full Name / Aliases:** Lord Alfric Danniken / "The Falconer"
- **Current Occupation:** Minor Noble / Head of the Cobalt Feather Syndicate.
- **Social Class / Standing:** Landed Citizen (Minor Nobility, purchased rather than inherited).
- **Primary Residence:** A sprawling, secluded countryside manor less than a day's ride from Port Nevarellon. He also maintains a modest, well-staffed townhouse in the city's Artisan Quarter for immediate business.
- **Affiliations:** [[100 Society/The Cobalt Feather Syndicate|The Cobalt Feather Syndicate]] (Founder).

## ⚖️ Realism & Physicality
- **Equipment & Upkeep:** Alfric dresses with impeccable but understated wealth. He shuns the heavy armor of the coastal Dukes, preferring tailored wool and silk. 
- **The Secret Aviary:** His wealth and minor title are entirely self-made, funded by his smuggling empire. At his country manor, he maintains a heavily guarded, secret aviary where he breeds a rare species of cobalt-plumed coastal raptors—the exclusive source of the Syndicate's identification tokens.

## 🧠 Psychology & Drive
- **Immediate Goal:** Expand his river-trade routes past the northern toll-keeps without triggering a turf war with the local Duchies.
- **The Core Fear:** A rogue agent committing a high-profile murder that forces the High Quarter magistrates to investigate his deeply buried financial ties to the criminal underworld.
- **Moral Compromises:** Alfric considers himself a civilized, merciful man because he forbids assassination. However, he is perfectly willing to destroy a rival merchant's life through blackmail, debt-traps, and extortion, leaving them to starve in the Sunken Ward.
