# Character: Tythius De Vonce
#cast/active #status/draft

> "The Duke stands as rigid as his keeps. He counts his life by the human kings he outlived, and he views the Council of Five as nothing more than low-born shopkeepers playing with stolen crowns." — Master Scribe Vaelen

## 📊 Vital Statistics
- **Full Name / Aliases:** Tythius De Vonce / "The Iron Duke" / "The Old Oak"
- **Current Occupation:** Sovereign Duke of [[The Five Duchies of the Whispering Coast#1. Duchy of De Vonce (The Iron Court)|The Duchy of De Vonce]]
- **Social Class / Standing:** High Nobility / Senior Signatory of the Ducal Accord
- **Primary Residence:** [[Castle Iron-Spire|Castle Iron-Spire]], Ancestral Seat of House De Vonce, northern foothills.
- **Affiliations:** House De Vonce (Patriarch); The Ducal Accord (Reluctant Protector).

## ⚖️ Realism & Physicality
- **Age & Vitality:** Half-Elf, 86 years old. Due to his elven heritage, he possesses the physical vitality of a human in his late 40s. He is exceptionally healthy, mentally sharp, and maintains a strict, military discipline.
- **Physical Flaws / Limitations:** He suffers from a deep, dull ache in his chest during mountain rainstorms—the legacy of an old crossbow bolt wound to the lung received during the final days of the Civil War. His elven ears are slightly blunted, a minor detail he hides beneath his hair to appear more human to his staunchly traditionalist vassals.
- **Financial Status:** Wealthy in terms of immovable assets, massive iron-ore deposits, and timber monopolies. However, his liquid gold reserves are constantly strained by the immense cost of maintaining a standing border levy along [[The Jagged Spine|The Jagged Spine]] to prevent another Corvus-style disaster.
- **Equipment & Upkeep:** Wears a highly polished, functional half-plate harness of blackened steel, meticulously oiled by his armorers to resist the damp coastal air. He carries *[[The Shard-Blade|The Shard-Blade]]*, an ancient, heavy longsword forged from iron pulled from the deep rifts—dense, heavy, and unyielding.

## 🧠 Psychology & Drive
- **Immediate Goal:** Secure an advantageous political marriage or treaty for his stubborn middle daughter, Sheandri, to lock down an alliance with the neighboring Duchy of Stonereach.
- **The Core Fear:** The extinction of his house through internal decay. He has watched human lines rot from within over his long life, and he deeply fears his children lack the hard iron grit required to protect the northern border when he finally passes.
- **Moral Compromises:** To fund his massive border keeps, Tythius turns a blind eye to the brutal working conditions within his iron mines. He also quietly permits certain "controlled" low-frequency alchemical weapons to be tested by his garrison captains, violating the spirit of the old laws for the sake of tactical readiness.

## 📜 Backstory & Current Role
Tythius was a young commander during the brutal Civil War that shattered the old kingdom. He personally witnessed the execution of the last King and was one of the original signatories who negotiated the Ducal Accord with the rising merchant class. Having lived for nearly nine decades, he has outlived all the original human merchants who hired the Golden Company. 

He treats Port Nevarellon with cold, calculated pragmatism. He despises the Council of Five, viewing them as greedy usurpers who ruined the natural feudal order. However, he is bound by the Accord. He frequently travels to the city's High Quarter to ensure the Council's maritime taxes continue to fund the protection of his duchy's borders, reminding them that if De Vonce falls to the wild inland, the city's trade will follow.
