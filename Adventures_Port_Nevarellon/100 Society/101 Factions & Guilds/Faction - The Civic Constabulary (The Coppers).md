# Faction: The Civic Constabulary (The Coppers)
#faction/active #status/solid

> "The Golden Company protects the gold. We protect the mud. And let me tell you, the mud doesn't pay its taxes on time, but it sure as hell stabs you just as deep." — Sergeant Vance, Dock-Watch

## 📊 Faction Profile
- **Formal Name:** The Civic Constabulary of Port Nevarellon
- **Street Names:** The Coppers, The Mud-Treaders, The Watch, Blue-Cloaks.
- **Type:** Local Municipal Law Enforcement / Toll Collectors.
- **Base of Operations:** The Toll-Houses (squat, reinforced stone blockhouses situated at the district borders and the edges of [[The Muddy Docks|The Muddy Docks]]).
- **Estimated Numbers:** ~400 active watchmen spread across the lower districts.

## ⚔️ Equipment & Logistics (The Realism Anchor)
- **The Pay Scale:** A watchman earns **6 to 8 cp per day**. It is slightly better than a dockhand's wage, but not nearly enough to risk dying against a Syndicate enforcer. Consequently, the Watch is deeply, systemically corrupt. Bribes are considered an unofficial part of their salary.
- **Weapons:** They are explicitly denied access to "High-Steel." They carry cast-iron truncheons, weighted leather saps, cheap harbor crossbows, and short-blades forged from reclaimed ship's bronze. 
- **Armor:** Hardened boiled-leather jacks, rusted ring-mail, and battered iron kettahats. They are visually identified by a tarnished copper badge pinned to their heavy wool  Blue cloaks. 
## ⚖️ Jurisdiction & Duties
The Constabulary is officially tasked with enforcing [[Law - The Council's Edicts|The Council's Edicts]] among the Un-Landed populace.
1.  **Toll Collection:** Their primary, daily duty is staffing the checkpoint gates, wringing the 1 cp vagrancy tolls out of laborers trying to cross districts.
2.  **The Palm-Length Edict:** They routinely stop and search dockhands for illegal blades, confiscating them (and often reselling them on the black market).
3.  **Meat-Shields:** When a major riot breaks out, the Golden Company uses the Constabulary as a vanguard to absorb the initial wave of bricks and crossbow bolts before the elite halberdiers move in.

## 🧠 Psychology & Internal Culture
- **Disillusioned Locals:** Almost all Coppers are Un-Landed citizens born in the slums. They joined the Watch to escape the grueling 14-hour shifts of the timber-yards. They are universally despised by their neighbors as class traitors.
- **Fear of the Syndicate:** The Watch avoids picking fights with the [[The Iron-Anchor Syndicate|Iron-Anchor Syndicate]]. A Watch sergeant knows that if he arrests one of [[Garrick the Keelhauler|Garrick's]] lieutenants, his own family will be burned out of their flophouse the next night. Therefore, they only arrest independent criminals, drunks, and those too poor to pay a bribe.
- **Hatred of the Golden Company:** The Coppers despise the Golden Company. The foreign mercenaries treat them like useless, undisciplined dogs, often pulling rank to humiliate local watchmen or overriding their arrests. 

## 🔗 Key Figures & Relations
- **Captain Elias "Half-Step" Vance:** *(NPC)* A grizzled, limping veteran of the Watch who runs the primary Toll-House in the Muddy Docks. He is firmly on Garrick's payroll, focusing his men's efforts entirely on petty theft and ignoring the massive smuggling galleons in the harbor.
- **Relation to Silas Bane:** [[Silas Bane|Silas]] specifically targets the Coppers. Because he lacks Garrick's deep pockets for bribes, Silas operates through terror, frequently leaving dead watchmen in alleyways to ensure the Constabulary is too terrified to patrol his territory at night.
