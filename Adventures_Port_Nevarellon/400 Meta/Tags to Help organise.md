Tags in Obsidian are best used for **status, traits, or cross-cutting attributes**, not for location. Because you are aiming for _fantasy-realism_, tags are incredibly useful for tracking the mechanical or physical state of things.

Here is a recommended tag framework:

### Realism & Status Tags

- `#status/draft` — Notes that are just rough ideas.
    
- `#status/solid` — Fact-checked world details you won't change.
    
- `#scale/local` — Restricted to Port Nevarellon.
    
- `#scale/global` — Affects the entire fantasy continent.
    

### Character/NPC Trait Tags

- `#cast/active` — Currently alive and in the area.
    
- `#cast/historical` — Dead, but left a legacy.
    
- `#faction/legal` — Officially recognized guilds.
    
- `#faction/underground` — Smugglers, rogue mages, syndicate
