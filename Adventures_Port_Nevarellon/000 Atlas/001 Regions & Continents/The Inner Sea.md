# Region: The Great Expanse (The Inner Sea)
#location/region #status/solid

> "The Expanse gives with one hand and takes with the other. She breeds monsters in her deep trenches, but shelters our ships with her barrier bones." — Captain Tessa, Leviathan Hunter

---

## 🗺️ Geography & Scope
- **Bordering Areas:** [[Port Nevarellon|Port Nevarellon]] (West), [[The Five Duchies of the Whispering Coast|The Whispering Coast]] (West), The Broken Ocean (East, beyond the barrier islands).
- **Terrain Type:** A vast, shallow-water inner sea. The far eastern boundary is defined by **The Shield Atolls**—a long, protective crescent string of volcanic islands, low-lying coral atolls, and shallow sandbars that completely shelter the inner basin from the cataclysmic open ocean storms of the Void.
- **Climate & Weather Patterns:** The inner basin remains remarkably calm and glassy for most of the year, enabling easy trade up and down the coast. However, during the deep **Winter Moons**, freezing winds sweep down from [[The Jagged Spine|The Jagged Spine]], bringing heavy, blinding maritime sleet and sudden, violent chop to the northern waters.

---

## 🪵 Logistics & Travel (The Realism Anchor)
- **Primary Routes:** 
  - **The Coastal Meridian:** The primary sea-lane running parallel to the mainland coast, completely clear of shoals.
  - **The Atoll Shallows:** A treacherous network of narrow, shifting deep-water channels cutting through the eastern barrier islands.
- **Travel Time to Port Nevarellon:** 
  - From the mainland coast: 4 to 12 hours by standard merchant galley, depending on coastal winds.
  - From the northern hunting township: 2 to 3 days sailing south-southwest under full canvas.
- **Hazards & Terrain Difficulties:** 
  - **The Sunken Ribs:** A massive stretch of shallow coral reefs near the central atolls that can rip the hull out of deep-draft vessels if they lack a local [[The Iron-Anchor Syndicate|Syndicate guide-boat]].
  - **Void-Currents:** Near the easternmost islands, the water behaves erratically, pulling toward unmapped rifts where the ocean shelf drops into the infinite abyss beneath the Disk.

---

## 🪓 Resource & Industry
- **Natural Resources:** Blubber oil (used for heating and machinery across Austhal), ambergris (a highly prized alchemical fixative for street lanterns), salted cod, sea-salt, and pristine black volcanic glass (obsidian) harvested from the southern atolls.
- **Settlements/Points of Interest:**
  - [[000 Atlas/Oakhaven Cove|Oakhaven Cove]] — *The Great Northern Township.* Located on the largest, northernmost shield island, built inside a high-walled, sheltered volcanic crater. It serves as the master port for the heavy, deep-sea monster-hunting fleets.
  - [[000 Atlas/The Shield Atolls|The Shield Atolls]] — *Sparsely populated micro-communities.* Dozens of tiny, isolated fishing villages made up of mixed mortal races (Humans, Halflings, and coastal Elves) living in stilt-houses built over the warm reefs.
  - [[000 Atlas/The Broken Spires|The Broken Spires]] — *Point of Interest.* A submerged ridge of petrified stone spires that match the architecture of the collapsed [[The Five Duchies of the Whispering Coast#5. The Scarred Land: Duchy of Corvus (The Fallen Crown)|Corvus Spire]], hinting that the ancient demonic incursion tore straight out into the sea.

---

## ⚔️ Power Dynamics & Threats
- **Who Claims It:** The **Council of Five** in Port Nevarellon claims legal authority over all commercial shipping and trade taxation within the inner sea basin.
- **Who Actually Controls It:** 
  - **The Inner Basin:** Shared control between independent merchant cartels and [[The Iron-Anchor Syndicate|The Iron-Anchor Syndicate]] (who run protection rackets on the coastal lanes).
  - **The Shield Islands:** Entirely self-governed by the local captains and village elders. The law of the islands is dictated by maritime necessity—the Golden Company has no standing army here.
- **Notable Fauna / Predators:** 
  - **Grounded Threats:** Reef-drakes and giant barnacle-mimics that latch onto wooden hulls to rot the timber.
  - **The Leviathans:** Massive, prehistoric, low-frequency monstrous sea creatures that drift up from the abyssal trenches of the outer ocean. Some are heavily armored, requiring specialized harpoon ballistas and alchemical explosives to kill.
