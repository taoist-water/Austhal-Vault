# District: The Great Anchor Basin
#location/district #status/solid

> "The Muddy Docks are where the city excretes its waste. The Basin is where it swallows its gold. If you drop a coin in the Basin, you don't hear a splash—you hear the clicking of a merchant's ledger." — Traditional Waterfront Saying

---

## 🗺️ District Overview
If [[The Muddy Docks]] represent the chaotic, rotting underbelly of Port Nevarellon's maritime trade, **The Great Anchor Basin** is its cold, clinical, heavily fortified heart. This massive, deep-water harbor was engineered during the early days of the old kingdom to accommodate deep-draft, ocean-going carracks and heavy military galleons from far-off continental empires. 

Unlike the sinking wooden boardwalks of the slums, the Basin is lined with massive, interlocking granite seawalls engineered with assistance from [[Ubaraz Kingdom]]. It commands the strategic gateway to the open ocean trade routes across [[000 Atlas/001 Regions & Continents/The Great Expanse|The Great Expanse]].

---

## 🪵 Logistics, Wealth & Security
- **The Paving Stones:** The Basin is strictly a **Landed Zone**. The streets are clean, wide enough for triple-hitched cargo wagons, and patrolled around the clock. 
- **The Toll Gates:** Un-Landed laborers are permitted into the Basin only during daylight hours if they carry a valid, Guild-stamped daily work-token. Passing through the Basin's iron checkpoints costs the standard **1 cp Vagrancy Toll**, a extraction that ensures the truly destitute are kept out.
- **The Security Force:** Security here is not left to the corrupt [[Faction - The Civic Constabulary (The Coppers)]]. The Basin is directly garrisoned by elite detachments of [[100 Society/101 Factions & Guilds/The Golden Company|The Golden Company]]. They enforce [[Law - The Council's Edicts]] with lethal precision—any Un-Landed laborer caught with a blade longer than a palm-width, or carrying a tool outside of working hours, is immediately stripped, beaten, and thrown into the harbor.

## ⚔️ The Underworld Friction
Because the Basin handles the highest-value cargo in the city (fine silks, spices, refined iron from De Vonce, and luxury alchemical reagents), it is an incredibly dangerous place to conduct illicit business. 

While [[The Iron-Anchor Syndicate]] dominates the low-end, blunt criminality of the Muddy Docks (protection rackets, extortion, timber smuggling), they find it incredibly difficult to operate within the Basin's high-security perimeter. This logistical vacuum has allowed a different kind of criminal entity to take root in the shadows of the granite seawalls: **[[The Cobalt Syndicate]]**.
