# Cosmology: The Celestial Graveyard & The War of Creation
#lore/cosmology #lore/history #status/solid

> "Look up at the sun and remember: we didn't just break the world. We left their corpses burning in the dark." — Archmage Vane

---

## ☀️ The Sky of Remnants: Sun and Moons
The celestial bodies drifting in the Void above the Great Disk are not astronomical spheres of gas or rock; they are the literal, glowing physical remains of the largest god-like entities.
- **The Sun:** The brilliant, burning corpse of a primary Creator entity. Its light is a fading, radiating echo of the original infinite energy that powered the Sphere.
- **The Moons:** The frozen, pale remnants of secondary entities. Because they are dead flesh drifting in the Void, they reflect the sun's fading energy, their cycles casting varying frequencies of light onto the Disk below.

---

## ⚔️ The Pre-Sundering War & The Birth of Monsters
Directly before the mortals executed their deicide, the cosmos experienced an **Explosion of Creation**—a desperate, violent cosmic civil war.

				[ THE ALLIANCE ] [ THE WEAPONIZERS ]

- Mortals (Human, Elf, Dwarf, Halfling) - Rogue Creator Gods
    
- Sympathetic Creator Gods - Manufactured Monstrous Races

1. **The Alliance:** Certain sympathetic creator gods actually sided with the mortal races, giving them the "science and magic" required to break the eternal cycle. 
2. **The Weaponizers:** In retaliation, the opposing gods violently accelerated their creative powers. They manufactured monstrous, hideous, and inherently destructive mortal beings specifically designed as shock-troops to slaughter both the mortals and the turncoat gods. 3. **The Legacy:** The "monstrous races" (orcs, goblins, chimeras, aberrations) are the descendants of these living weapons. They were engineered for war, chaos, and entropy, which is why they are biologically and psychologically driven toward destruction. 
## 🎚️ The Frequency Aftermath: 
Archetypal Realities When the mortals finally succeeded in destroying the gods and the Sphere shattered, the newly formed parallel realities didn't just scatter randomly. They were shaped by **mortal perception**. Because mortal consciousness had undergone *individuation*, their collective fears, guilt, hopes, and beliefs acted as a mold for the escaping god-essences: 
- **The Higher Frequencies (The Heavens):** God-essence that aligned with order, light, and the mortal hope for salvation shifted into higher frequencies, solidifying into the archetypal realms of "Heaven." 
- **The Lower Frequencies (The Hells):** The violent, corrupt god-essences, combined with the collective mortal terror of punishment and the dark nature of the monstrous weapons, sank into the lowest frequencies, solidifying into "Hell." Because these are just points on a frequency spectrum, they are technically accessible if a mortal can find a way to shift their energy or physical matter to match that specific frequency—though doing so without a stabilizing anchor is usually fatal. 
## ⚓ The Realism Intersect for Port Nevarellon 
### 1. The Corrupting Light of the Moon
Because the moons are the corpses of gods weaponized or shattered in the ancient war, their light isn't clean. When the moons pass through specific low-frequency alignments above the Disk, they stir the dormant, genetic programming of the monstrous races outside the city walls, making predators more aggressive and causing the "lower frequency" alchemical mixtures in the docks (like **Brine-Fire**) to become highly volatile. 
### 2. The High Quarter's Divine Right 
The elites in **The High Quarter** base their entire legal and moral authority on the claim that they can tap into the "Higher Order Frequencies" (The Heavens). They view the lower classes in the docks as structurally closer to the "Hell" frequencies because the dregs live surrounded by filth, crime, and rotting environment. 
### 3. The Ruins of the War 
Because the disk is so vast and the war was fought everywhere before the shattering, if one digs deep enough into the rock beneath Port Nevarellon or explores the wilderness of [[The Whispering Coast]], they won't find traditional dinosaur fossils. They will find the titanic, petrified bones of manufactured monsters and fallen gods from the Pre-Sundering War, often still bleeding raw, unanchored magical energy into the stone.


# Cosmology: The Great Fracture & The Infinite Disk
#lore/cosmology #lore/history #status/solid

> "We killed the anchors of the world to steal their sky. Now we drift on a broken plate, looking out into nothing." — Ancient Elven Fragment, Year Unknown

---

## 🌌 The Primordial State: The Sphere
Before history began, existence was a perfect, closed, three-dimensional system—**The Sphere**. 
- There was no beginning, no end, and no decay. It was a flawless perpetual motion machine of reality.
- **The Creators:** Immense, nameless entities (whom mortals now call Gods) built this closed system. For them, the creation and maintenance of the Sphere *was* their ultimate purpose. 
- **The Experiment:** To test the limits of this infinite loop, the entities created the four mortal races—**Humans, Elves, Dwarves, and Halflings**—each entity designing a variant to see if any mortal could find a hidden beginning or end within the eternal cycle.

## 🩸 The Corruption of Individuation
A catastrophic, unforeseen byproduct occurred when mortal life was introduced: **Individuation**.
- As mortals began to experience distinct, separate lives, their consciousness fractured away from the unity of the universe.
- This fragmentation birthed petty, volatile mortal emotions (greed, envy, ambition, fear). 
- Because the creators were linked to their creations, this emotional rot bled upward. The mortal races unconsciously *infected* the gods with their own psychological flaws.

## 🔨 The Deicide & The Shattering
Once infected with ambition and fear, the mortal races discovered the immortality and magic/science of their creators. Driven by a desire for absolute freedom or power, the mortals weaponized this stolen knowledge and **destroyed the Gods**.

The weight of this deicide tore the fabric of reality apart:
1. **The Sphere holding existence together collapsed.** 
2. It was violently reshaped into **The Great Disk**: a flat plane of physical matter so staggeringly vast that no known mortal has ever found its outer edge, nor dug deep enough to find the sky at the bottom.
3. The surrounding infinity became **The Void**: an absolute nothingness in which the Disk drifts endlessly.

---

## 📻 The Spectrum of Reality: Frequencies of God-Essence
The gods were not completely erased; they were chaotically shattered into innumerable essences. Their deaths created the multi-planar spectrum that defines your magic system:

[ HIGHER FREQUENCIES ] --> Order, Law, Light, "Good" 
| [ THE MORTAL PLANE ] --> The Great Disk (Physical Anchor) |
[ LOWER FREQUENCIES ] --> Chaos, Decay, Entropy, "Evil"

- **The Shifted Realities:** When the gods shattered, pieces of their essence shifted out of phase, creating entirely separate realities that exist parallel to the mortal plane, separated only by a shift in **frequency**. 
- **The Tether:** A fundamental strand of god-essence remains anchored in the physical mortal plane. This anchor holds all the other shifted realities together like notes on a musical scale, stretching from the highest frequencies (Absolute Law/Good) to the lowest frequencies (Absolute Chaos/Evil). 
## ⚓ How the Macro-Cosmos Impacts Port Nevarellon (The Realism Intersect) 
Because your world is built on *fantasy-realism*, this cosmic origin story directly forces specific physical laws down onto the lower-class dregs of your harbor city:
### 1. The Scarcity of High-Order Elements 
Because the gods are shattered across a spectrum, pure "magic" is actually the manipulation of these frequencies. 
- Lower frequencies (chaos, rot, brine-fire) are heavy, volatile, and easy to access on the muddy docks. 
- Higher frequencies (pure law, clean energy) are incredibly rare, expensive, and heavily monopolized by the elite in **The High Quarter**.
